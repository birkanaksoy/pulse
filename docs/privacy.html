Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

Everything runs on your device

Pulse performs every measurement locally using iOS APIs. Storage, thermal state, battery level, charging state, Low Power Mode, photo counts — all of this is read by your iPhone and stays on it. Nothing is uploaded.

What we never collect

No analytics SDK, no advertising identifier, no crash-reporting third party, no usage tracking. We do not ask for your email or any account.

Photo Library access

If you grant Photo Library access, Pulse reads only metadata (counts and file sizes) to populate the Clean tab. Your photos and videos never leave your device.

Subscriptions

Pulse Pro is handled by Apple via StoreKit. We receive only an entitlement flag (Pro: yes/no) from Apple — never your payment details.

Notifications

If you enable the weekly check-in, Pulse schedules a local notification on your device. No notification content leaves the phone.

Your control

Settings → Privacy → Delete all data wipes every scan record, the widget snapshot, and the weekly reminder in one step.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@pulseapp.app.
