Frequently asked

Why doesn't Pulse show my real battery health %?

iOS does not expose true battery health to third-party apps. Tap "See Battery Health" inside Pulse to jump to Apple's official page in Settings.

Does Pulse delete my photos?

Never. Every cleanup action opens the relevant Apple app so you decide.

What does Pulse send to the internet?

Nothing for the core app. The only network call is StoreKit's subscription verification, handled by Apple directly.

How do I cancel Pulse Pro?

iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Pulse Pro → Cancel.
